Pizza Recipe

Ingrediants
1 1/2 cups warm water
1/4 spoon dry yeast
3 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
2tbsp of Olive oil
pinch of sugar

Preparation
preheat the ovn at 280 c.place the warm water in the large bowl of a heavy duty stand mixer.sprinkle the yeast over the warm water and let it sit for 5 min until the yeast dissolves and place aplastic cover and let the dough raise 2 times of the original
using the roler make the dough as tortila and add ur favoute veggies,meat and topping and let the dough be in ovn fo 20 min
take out the pizza and let it cool down 
Enjoy your pizza
